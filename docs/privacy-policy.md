# Lumina Tag Explorer Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Lumina Tag Explorer is a local Windows desktop file-tagging application.

The app can access files and folders that you choose so it can display, tag, rename, copy, move, delete, and organize local files. This information stays on your device.

Lumina Tag Explorer does not upload your files, file names, folder paths, tags, or settings to the developer or to any third-party service.

The app stores its settings locally on your device, including saved locations, tag groups, and view preferences. You can remove this local data by uninstalling the app or deleting its local app data.

Lumina Tag Explorer does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, telemetry, or cloud sync.

For support, contact the developer through the project repository:
https://github.com/FelixHenrikChristian/Lumina
